Welcome to Your Beauty and Skincare Guide

Skin Types and Recommendations

  • Oily Skin: Use lightweight, oil-free moisturizers and clay-based masks.
  • Dry Skin: Opt for rich creams and hydrating masks with hyaluronic acid.
  • Combination Skin: Balance with gel moisturizers and masks targeting different zones.
  • Sensitive Skin: Choose fragrance-free, calming products with aloe vera or chamomile.
  • Normal Skin: Maintain with balanced products and occasional hydrating masks.

Makeup Tips

  • Foundation: Choose a foundation matching your skin tone and type (matte for oily skin, hydrating for dry skin).
  • Mascara: Opt for volumizing mascara for fuller lashes, or lengthening for added definition.
  • Lipstick: Hydrating lipsticks are ideal for dry lips, while matte lipsticks provide a long-lasting finish.
  • Blush: Use powder blush for oily skin and cream blush for dry or normal skin.
  • Eyeshadow: Neutral shades suit daily use, while bold colors are great for special occasions.
